Sat Paul Mittal School

Sports

The students learn skating, table-tennis, football, basketball, swimming, dodge ball, badminton and participate in various athletic events throughout the year. Hundred percent participation is the key attraction of Annual Sports Day. The School Sports Carnival offers equal opportunities to parents and grandparents. Run for Elders is organized for the Grandparents to commemorate World Elders’ Day in October every year. Besides regular games period, the students are trained by experienced coaches, after school. The school offers free transport facility to the budding Sporty Satyans.Our students also participate and win various Inter-School Competition, at district, state and national level.
